Privacy Policy

HeartBeep — Heart Rate Monitor

Overview

HeartBeep is a heart rate monitoring app that connects to Bluetooth Low Energy heart rate sensors (such as Polar H10) to track your heart rate during exercise. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built: all data stays on your device, and the app makes zero network calls.

Data Collected

During a monitoring session the app may record the following data:

  • Heart rate — real-time readings from your connected BLE sensor
  • Session duration — start time, end time, and elapsed time of each session
  • GPS distance — distance travelled during a session (if location permission is granted)
  • Session history — a log of past sessions with summary statistics

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. HeartBeep does not transmit any data over the internet. There are no analytics services, no crash reporting tools, no third-party SDKs, and no remote servers involved. Your data never leaves your phone.

Permissions

HeartBeep requests only the permissions required for core functionality:

  • Bluetooth / Nearby Devices — required to discover and connect to your BLE heart rate sensor.
  • Location (optional) — used to track GPS distance during exercise sessions. Also required by Android for BLE scanning on some OS versions. Location data is never transmitted anywhere.
  • Foreground Service / Notifications — allows heart rate monitoring to continue while the screen is off or another app is in the foreground.
  • Vibration — used for haptic feedback on certain UI interactions.

Third-Party Services

HeartBeep uses no third-party services. There is no advertising, no analytics, no cloud storage, and no data sharing of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
