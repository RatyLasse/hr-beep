## App Name
HeartBeep

## Short Description (max 80 characters)
Live heart rate alerts from your Bluetooth sensor. Stay in the zone.

## Full Description (max 4000 characters)
HeartBeep turns your phone into a live heart rate monitor with smart alerts that tell you the instant your heart rate goes too high or too low.

Connect any standard Bluetooth LE heart rate sensor -- like a Polar H10 -- and HeartBeep gives you a large, glanceable heart rate display with a real-time scrolling graph. Set an upper threshold, a lower threshold, or both, and the app will beep the moment your heart rate crosses either boundary. The alarm tone matches your actual heart rate rhythm, so you always know exactly what is happening without looking at your phone.


REAL-TIME MONITORING

Your current heart rate is shown in a large, bold readout over a live 60-sample graph. The display turns green when you are within your target zone and red when you are outside it. The graph and readout update continuously as long as your sensor is connected.


CONFIGURABLE BPM ALERTS

Set upper and lower heart rate thresholds to define your target zone. When your heart rate crosses either boundary, HeartBeep plays a distinctive alarm tone -- a higher pitch for exceeding the upper limit and a lower pitch for dropping below the lower limit. The beep cadence matches your current heart rate so you can gauge intensity by sound alone. Adjust alarm volume independently with the sound intensity control.


GPS DISTANCE AND PACE TRACKING

Enable optional GPS tracking during sessions to record distance and pace. HeartBeep announces each kilometre aloud so you can stay focused on your activity. Live pace is displayed in the session stats alongside your heart rate data.


SPOKEN AUDIO ALERTS

HeartBeep uses text-to-speech to announce when your sensor connects or disconnects mid-session, plus distance milestones during GPS-tracked sessions. You will never miss an important event, even when your phone is in your pocket.


SESSION HISTORY WITH GRAPHS

Every completed session is saved on your device and shown in a browsable history tab. Each session card displays start time, duration, average heart rate, distance, pace, and a miniature heart rate graph. Delete sessions you no longer need with swipe-to-delete and undo support.


TCX EXPORT

Export your full session history as a standard TCX file for use with Garmin Connect, Strava, TrainingPeaks, or any fitness platform that accepts TCX imports. Each exported session includes timestamped heart rate trackpoints, average and max heart rate, duration, and distance.


RUNS IN THE BACKGROUND

Monitoring continues reliably when your screen is off. HeartBeep uses a foreground service with a persistent notification so your session keeps running during your workout. The app can also wake your screen when an alert fires.


WORKS WITH ANY BLE HEART RATE SENSOR

HeartBeep supports any heart rate monitor that implements the standard Bluetooth Low Energy Heart Rate Service profile. Developed and tested with the Polar H10, but compatible with chest straps and arm bands from Garmin, Wahoo, CooSpo, Magene, and others. Battery level from your sensor is displayed in the app.


YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE

HeartBeep has no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no tracking. All your session data and settings are stored locally on your phone. Your heart rate data belongs to you and only you.
